Cold Cured Trout

Pan-seared salmon

This cold-cured trout highlights the richness of Scandinavian rivers, with juniper berries, dill, and an array of spices infusing its flavor.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound (450g) fresh salmon fillet, boneless and skin-on
  • 2 tablespoons coarse sea salt
  • 2 tablespoons granulated sugar
  • 1 tablespoon crushed juniper berries
  • 1 tablespoon finely chopped fresh dill
  • Zest of 1 lemon
  • Zest of 1 orange
  • A clean, food-safe container or dish
  • Plastic wrap or aluminum foil

Steps

  1. Prepare the Salmon
  2. Mix the Cure
  3. Apply the Cure
  4. Wrap and Refrigerate
  5. Cure in the Refrigerator for 2-3 days
  6. Slice and Serve
